Q7 - Functions + Conditionals (2 points)#

Background: Students are often curious about their grade in classes. Here, you’re going to help students figure out their grade by writing a function.

You need to define a function called “check_grade” by taking in one parameter called score, attendance.

The status of a student will only be shown if attendance is greater than 75, otherwise your grade would be displayed as ‘F’. Considering that a student has their attendance greater than 75, ‘A’ grade is given to students who have a score of 90+, ‘B’ grade is given to students who have a score of 75-90, ‘C’ grade is given to students who have a score of 60-75, and if less than 60, ‘D’ grade is given.

Find the output, of a student (grade of that student) given the attendance and the score.

### BEGIN SOLUTION
def check_grade(score, attendance):
    if attendance < 75:
        grade = 'F'
    else:
        if score > 90:
            grade = 'A'
        elif score >75:
            grade = 'B'
        elif score >60:
            grade = 'C'
        else:
            grade = 'D'
    
    return grade
### END SOLUTION

### BEGIN HIDDEN TESTS
